WELCOME TO Shaik-Foods

Shaik-Foods is an Indian multinational restaurant aggregator and food delivery company founded by Deepinder Goyal and Pankaj Chaddah in 2022. Shaik-Foods provides information, menus and user-reviews of restaurants as well as food delivery options from partner restaurants in select cities.
